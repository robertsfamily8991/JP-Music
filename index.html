Is It Worth Repairing

Our most frequently asked question, "Is it worth repairing?" Many of our customers ask us, and the best answer that we can provide is... "It depends!" We’ll give you our honest opinion up front. Sometimes repairing a lower priced handheld tool such as a weed eater, leaf blower, lawn mower, chainsaw or snow blower from certain manufacturers can cost nearly as much as a new one, but keep in mind - that often times a seasoned and well maintained lawn tool will perform better than a brand-new one. We’re here to answer any questions you might have and will gladly walk you through any process needed to keep your equipment running smoothly for years to come.
